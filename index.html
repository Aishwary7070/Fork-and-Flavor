PIZZA

RECIPES INGREDIENTS

  • 1 1/2 Cups Tomato Sauce or Crushed Tomatoes

  • 2 Cloves of Garlic

  • 1 Tablespoon Olive Oil

  • 1 Teaspoon Salt

  • 1/2 Teaspoon Sugar (Optional)

  • 1 Teaspoon Dried Oregano

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Active the Yeast:
    • In a small bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Stir gently and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Make the Dough:
    • In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt.
    • Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the Dough:
    • Transfer the dough to a lightly floured surface. Knead for about 8-10 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Let it Rise:
    • place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has double in size.
  5. Prepare for Baking:
    • Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (usually around 475°F or 245°C) and place a pizza stone or an inverted baking sheet inside to heat up.
